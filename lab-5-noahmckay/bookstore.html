Hello, and welcome to The Book Nook!

We are a local family business that opened their doors in 2020. We strongly believe that books are knowledge, and that it should be accessible for everyone. In 2022 we noticed a lot of librarys had closed, and started the Reading Room addition to our store. We accept gently used books, in exchange for a coupon for our store to get a new read! The books in the Reading Room are free of charge, and yours to keep or trade in.
The Book Nook is located in Happy Valley-Goose Bay, Newfoundland. We are passionate about serving our local community, our Reading Room is only available locally however our store is open in person and online. Go to our Contact Us Page for hours and contact information.
