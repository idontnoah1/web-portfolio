Laura Jane Grace


Laura Jane Grace's Website

I like Laura Jane Grace because she is a trans punk rock artist that brought awareness and music about trans issues to the mainstream media. She played punk-rock music with the band Against Me!, as well as solo work, and is currently touring with the Trauma Tropes. I have gone to her concerts 3 times, and she is very good live.

My favourite songs are:

  • Black Me Out
  • True Trans Soul Rebel
  • Paralytic States
  • I Was A Teenage Anarchist
  • Hole In My Head
