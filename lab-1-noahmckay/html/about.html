Laura Jane Grace

About Laura Jane Grace

She has 2 solo albums, as well as 7 albums with Against Me!, and an album in Laura Jane Grace and the Devouring Mothers, as well as Laura Jane Grace in the Trauma Tropes. She has published one memoir book in 2016.

In 2012, Laura Jane Grace publicly came out as transgender and began transitioning. She is one of the most visible trans musicians and advocates for LGBTQ2S+ rights.
